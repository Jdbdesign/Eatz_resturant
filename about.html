How It Started

From a home kitchen
to thousands of tables

It started with a craving and a tiny kitchen. Our founder, driven by the desire to share the joy of exceptional food, began crafting dishes that combined international technique with local love. Word spread fast.

Five years on, Eatz operates across three cities, employs 18 talented chefs, and serves thousands of families every week. But our heart? It's still in that tiny kitchen where it all began.

Frequently Asked
Questions

Everything you need to know about dining and ordering from Eatz.

Eatz began in 2019 with our founder cooking out of a home kitchen in Lagos, driven by a passion for making gourmet food accessible. After growing through word-of-mouth and a small online presence, we expanded to three cities and over 18 chefs in just five years.
We work exclusively with certified local farms and trusted regional suppliers. All proteins are free-range or sustainably sourced, produce is seasonal, and we never use artificial preservatives. Our head chef approves every new supplier personally.
Absolutely. We have a dedicated catering division that handles weddings, corporate events, birthdays, and galas. We can cater for groups from 20 to 2,000 guests with fully customised menus and on-site chef service. Reach out through our Contact page to get a bespoke quote.
Yes! We offer exclusive private dining experiences at all three locations. Book through our Reservation page and select your group size, or contact us directly for fully bespoke experiences — from chef's table dinners to themed tasting menus.
We take dietary needs seriously. Our menu features clearly labelled vegetarian, vegan, and gluten-free options across every category. We also cater to allergies — just add a note at checkout or call us before ordering and our team will guide you through the safest choices.
